Printed parts:
-Nema17_5to1_mount.stl		Stepper holder for cutting. Drawn to support/mount 5:1 geared stepper
-partA_5-1_geared.stl		Shaft attachment for cutting stepper
-StopSwitchDual.stl		Limit switch(s) mount that attaches to Nema17_5to1_mount.
-limitSW_guide.stl		Cover for limit switch to prevent vertical bending.
-Cut_Link.stl			Link piece attaches to partA.
-Link_Spacer.stl		Spacer from Cut_Link to actual cutter.
-cutter_holder_adj.stl		Mount for actual cutter.
-wire_guide_BOT.stl		Guide/mount for wire tube to cutter 
-wire_guide_TOP.stl		Guide/mount for wire tube to cutter 
-Feed_Stand.stl			Stepper holder for wire feed stepper.
-Display_Stand.stl		Crude holder for Nextion 5" screen.
-Display_Bezel.stl		Bezel for Display_Stand
